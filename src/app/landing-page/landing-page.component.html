ICAT Transport Climate Action Data Tool (TraCAD)

About the tool

The TraCAD tool will provide a more consistent approach to data collection and assessment, which will improve the credibility of impact assessments of policies and actions. TraCAD has 4 main functionalities :
Data Management

All activities from data entry to quality control can be done in the tool. The tool can link the multiple stakeholders involved in the data collection process, and collect a wide range of data and information on climate actions.




Greenhouse gas Impact Assessment

The tool can conduct greenhouse gas impact assessment using both Ex-ante and Ex-post approaches based on international methodologies.




Marginal Abatement Cost (or MAC) Assessment

The tool can calculate the costs associated with selected climate actions and generates a MAC curve. This helps identify the climate actions providing the least and most emission reductions, and what their cost is.




Tracking Climate Actions

The Tool can facilitate regular monitoring and tracking the progress of specific climate actions throughout their life cycle. Tailored reports can be generated and adjusted according to country reporting needs.




Methodologies

The Tool includes different methodologies developed by ICAT, CDM, JICA to assess the GHG impact of different specific climate actions.
