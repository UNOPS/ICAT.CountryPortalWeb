ICAT Transport Climate Action Data Tool (TraCAD)

About the tool

TraCAD supports and facilitates countries to systematically assess the impact of climate actions in the Transport sector. It does this by streamlining all aspects of the data collection process, and offers standard methodologies, calculations and reporting in one place. TraCAD has 4 main functionalities:
Data collection and management

All activities from data entry to quality control can be done in TraCAD. The tool can link the multiple stakeholders involved in the data collection process, and collect a wide range of data and information on climate actions.


GHG impact assessment

TraCAD can conduct greenhouse gas impact assessments using both Ex-ante and Ex-post approaches based on international methodologies. Currently, TraCAD contains over 20 methodologies such as those developed by ICAT, the Clean Development Mechanism (CDM) and the Japanese International Cooperation Agency (JICA).


Marginal abatement cost (MAC) assessment

TraCAD can calculate the costs associated with selected climate actions and generate a MAC curve. This can facilitate countries to identify the climate actions providing the least and most emission reductions, and what the associated cost of the climate action is.


Tracking climate actions

TraCAD can facilitate regular monitoring and tracking of specific climate actions throughout their life cycle. Tailored reports can be generated and adjusted according to country reporting needs.



Get started with TraCAD

TraCAD is an open source software and is free to use. If you are interested in using TraCAD and wish to learn more about the tool and its potential application, please contact the ICAT Secretariat: icat@unops.org
